Katsiaryna Banar

General

Katsiaryna Banar, born in small town in Belarus, 1993.
Currently I am a student at Royal Academy of Arts, the Hague.

The starting point of any of my projects is theory that inflates design and research appears to be the main core for any of my projects.

I work with different mediums, there is no specific preference as I believe the awareness of the content is the most important and it dictates the further implementations of elements with the most suitable usage of any medium. Process and research plays very important role on the way of determining the final result.

I work a lot with text and language. For example, Amnesty International case is dedicated fully to work with language in such documents as Constitution of the People’s Republic of China, Criminal Verdict of Liu Xiaobo, Charter 08 and other articles of Liu Xiaobo on freedom (go to project).
