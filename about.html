Katsiaryna Banar

General

Katsiaryna Banar, born in a small town in Belarus, 1993.
Currently I am a student at Royal Academy of Arts, the Hague.

In my works I put much effort in theory that inflates the design.

The awareness of the content dictates the further implementations with the most suitable usage of the medium. I do not have any specific preference, as the research appears to be the main core for any of my projects.

I like working with text and language, combining different sources of information and driving conclusions.See example.

I feel most confident working in spatial environment, creating new narratives based on various inputs. See example.

I believe that design could be used as a tool to investigate and fight abuses of human rights. See example.
